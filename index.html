About HTS2S

Assisting the Youth never doubt that they are Valuable & Powerful

HTS2S facilitates events and courses that provides families with information and skills, advocate for policy and procedure changes, provides incentives to youth, as well as promote and support community resources in order to promote youth substance use prevention.
